But anyways back to Pickleball!

There's so many different ways to play!

  1. Singles
    • Perfect for you and a friend
    • It's great for getting in a quick workout
    • No need to worry about organizing a big group
  2. Doubles
    • Amazing socializing activity
    • A little less energy intensive then singles, but still a great workout
  3. Cut Throat
    • Played with three people, and the person playing alone is the only one who can score
    • Anytime that person loses a point, everyone rotates
    • First to eleven wins!
